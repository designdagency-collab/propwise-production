What is upblock.ai?

upblock.ai is an AI-powered property research platform designed for Australian homeowners, investors, and property professionals. Enter any Australian address and receive a comprehensive property intelligence report in seconds — no signup required.

Our platform analyzes zoning regulations, planning overlays, comparable sales data, and development potential to help you identify hidden opportunities and make smarter property decisions.

  • Development Potential – Discover subdivision, dual occupancy, and multi-dwelling opportunities
